Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

At Compassionate Care Management, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information obtained through our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, telephone number, and any information you submit through our contact forms. We may also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your questions and requests.
  • Provide information about our healthcare management services.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Communicate with you regarding services you have requested.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

Protection of Your Information

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the personal information we collect. While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

HIPAA Notice

Please do not submit confidential medical information or Protected Health Information (PHI) through this website or its contact forms unless specifically instructed to do so through a secure, HIPAA-compliant communication method.

Visiting this website or contacting us through the website does not establish a provider-patient relationship.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and improve our services. You may adjust your browser settings to disable cookies if you choose.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or use third-party services, including analytics, maps, scheduling platforms, or social media. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party services.

Information Sharing

Compassionate Care Management does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only when required by law or with trusted service providers who assist in operating our business and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
