Meet Our Team

Behind every plan of care is a team that genuinely cares. Get to know the people dedicated to guiding, caring for, and supporting you.

Lisa Fuentes-Tidona

Partner · Director of Business Development · Business Management Specialist

Lisa brings a passion for healthcare innovation, patient advocacy, and strategic relationship building. She works closely with physicians, healthcare organizations, and community partners to expand access to high-quality, patient-centered care.

Focus: Building trusted partnerships that connect patients with compassionate, coordinated care.

Treating every patient with compassion, dignity, and personalized support while improving health outcomes and quality of life.

Rajni Roy, MSN, APRN, FNP-C, CHPN

Partner · Director of Operations Management

Rajni is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care that improves health outcomes. She is experienced in primary care, chronic disease management, case management, and long-term care.

Focus: Delivering personalized, evidence-based care while helping patients achieve healthier, more independent lives.

I care about treating every patient with compassion, dignity, and respect while supporting individuals and families through every stage of their healthcare journey.

Chinasa Chudi-Attah, MSN, APRN, FNP-C, CHPN

Partner · Director of Clinical Operations Management

Chinasa is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner committed to delivering compassionate, evidence-based care that supports the health and well-being of every patient. With experience in primary care, acute and chronic disease management, home care, hospice, and palliative care, she provides comprehensive, patient-centered care across every stage of life.

Focus: Preventive care, chronic disease management, and empowering patients through education, collaboration, and personalized care plans.

I believe every patient deserves to be heard, respected, and cared for with compassion. My goal is to help individuals and families achieve the best possible quality of life through personalized, holistic care.
